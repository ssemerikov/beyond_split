## What not to write

- No identifying details about Sp1–Sp4 beyond the role label. Specifically:
  no university names, no titles, no city of work.
- No verbatim Ukrainian quotation in the body text. All transcript material is
  paraphrased into English; the original is preserved in
  `transcripts/transcript_quotes.md` for the authors' records and may be
  reproduced in a supplementary file at the editor's discretion.
- No claim that the round-table is representative of Ukrainian academic
  opinion. It is one expert deliberation. Triangulate every claim with the
  WoS corpus or a primary document before including it in the manuscript.
